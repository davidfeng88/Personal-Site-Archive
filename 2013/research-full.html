Graduate Work

  • Studying phosphine-sulfonate (PO) palladium alkyl complexes, which can catalyze copolymerization of ethylene and polar monomers to form functionalized linear polyethylenes.

  • My research project involves

    • Organic and organometallic synthesis and characterizations (NMR, GC-MS, ESI-MS);

    • Ethylene polymerization and polymer characterizations (GPC, DSC, NMR);

    • DFT calculations.

Undergraduate Work

  • In Prof. Yan Li's group at Peking University:

    • Developed a new ionic-liquid-assisted method to prepare carbon nanotube / platinum nano-particle complexes, in which the platinum nano-particles were dispersed much more uniformly compared to samples prepared by conventional polyol reduction method.

    • Employed the complexes as the anode catalyst in direct methanol fuel cells and amperometric detector of hydrogen peroxide.

    • Publication:
      Haibin Chu; Yihong Shen; Liang Lin; Xiaojun Qin; Ge Feng; Ziyin Lin; Jinyong Wang; Haichao Liu; Yan Li. Adv. Funct. Mater. 2010, 20, 3747-3752.

    • Patent:
      Haibin Chu; Yan Li; Jinyong Wang; Ge Feng; Liang Lin; Ziyin Lin. Carbon nanotube and metal nano-particle complexes and synthetic method thereof. CN 101417796, Apr 29, 2009.

  • In Prof. Anne McNeil's group at University of Michigan during summer, 2008 (UM/PKU exchange program):

    • Studied palladium transfer in Suzuki couplings.
